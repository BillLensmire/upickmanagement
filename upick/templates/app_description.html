About You Pick Manager

You Pick Manager [A free and opensource Django application]

You Pick Manager was written by Bill Lensmire. Bill grew up on a farm in northeast Wisconsin (Kewaunee County) and has a degree in Computer Science/Mathematics from Carroll College. (This was in the days of mainframe computers and 8086/8088 CPUs...now you know how old I am :) Like many of you, Bill spent many years developing software in a Dilbert cubical for companies like GE Medical Systems and Agfa Healthcare. Bill along with his wife have a small farm in southern Wisconsin (zone 5b) where they grow vegetables for their local community.

Why Opensource?

  • Like many of you who follow the teachings of Jesus, the Son of God, I feel compelled to share the fruits of my labor with others. I believe that opensource software is one way to share these fruits with others.
  • I want to help you to spend less time on the computer and more time in your garden growing food.
  • We need more people like you growing fresh, healthy, chemical-free (no pesticides, herbicides, or fungicides) food for our local neighbors and communities.

Jesus said "Love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind and with all your strength. The second is this: Love your neighbor as yourself. There is no commandment greater than these."

Available Features:
  • ToDo Lists and Tasks
  • Plant & Variety Management
  • Garden Bed Management
  • Produce Harvest Planning
  • Indoor Seed Planting and Transplanting date management
  • Calendar with PDF Export
  • Beneficials
  • Garden NoteLog with Photo and PDF upload
Future Features:

  • Comprehensive reporting
Why You Pick Manager?

Bill wrote You Pick Manager because he was tired of using excel to plan and manage his U Pick Market Garden. Excel is a great tool, but it becomes brittle and error-prone when you have a lot of data and complex relationships. You Pick Manager is a web application that allows you to plan and manage your U Pick Market Garden in a more efficient and error-free way.

Bill wrote this software with help from AI in 2025. The complexity of creating a modern web app is too great for a single developer to handle.

Experience the power and efficiency of streamlined management with You Pick Manager - your partner in operational excellence.
